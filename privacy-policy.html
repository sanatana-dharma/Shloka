Privacy Policy for Shloka App

Effective Date: February 15, 2026

1. Introduction

Sanatana Dharma ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Shloka mobile application (the "App" or "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we process, why we process it, and your choices.

2. Information We Process

2.1 Spiritual Content and App Preferences

The App downloads spiritual content (such as shlokas, chapters, and related data) and stores content and settings locally on your device for app functionality.

2.2 Subscription and Entitlement Data (RevenueCat + App Stores)

If you use paid features, we process limited subscription-related data through app stores and RevenueCat to validate purchases and manage access.

  • Pseudonymous app user ID (for example, values like usr_...)
  • Subscription product ID and entitlement status (active/expired/etc.)
  • Purchase lifecycle event data (renewal, cancellation, expiration, refund/revoke)
  • Store/environment metadata (for example, App Store / Sandbox / Production)
  • Technical request metadata needed for security, reliability, and abuse prevention

We do not require your name, phone number, or home address to use the App.

2.3 Authentication and Security Data

To protect gated content and service integrity, our backend may process session/authentication tokens and service logs necessary for security and troubleshooting.

Important: We do not use your data for third-party advertising and we do not sell personal data.

3. How We Use Information

  • Deliver and update spiritual content
  • Enable and enforce subscription access
  • Restore purchases and handle billing-state changes
  • Maintain service reliability, performance, and security
  • Investigate misuse, fraud, or technical issues
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

4. Legal Bases (Where Applicable)
