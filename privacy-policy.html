3. Information We Process

The App downloads spiritual content (such as shlokas, chapters, and related data) and stores content and settings locally on your device for app functionality. We do not receive or access the locally stored content on your device.

3.2 Subscription and Entitlement Data (App Stores + RevenueCat)

If you use paid features, we process limited subscription-related data through app stores and RevenueCat to validate purchases and manage access.

  • Pseudonymous app user identifier (for example, values like usr_...)

  • Purchase lifecycle event data (renewal, cancellation, expiration, refund/revoke)
  • Store/environment metadata (for example, App Store / Sandbox / Production)
  • Technical request metadata needed for security, reliability, and abuse prevention

We do not collect or require your name, username, email address, phone number, or home address to use the App. We do not provide such information to RevenueCat or the app stores.

To protect gated content and service integrity, our backend may process session/authentication tokens and service logs necessary for security, abuse prevention, and troubleshooting.

Important: We do not use your data for third-party advertising and we do not sell personal data.
