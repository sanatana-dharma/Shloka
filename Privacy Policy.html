Privacy Policy for Shloka App

Effective Date: September 21, 2025

Introduction

Sanatana Dharma ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Shloka mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Information Collection and Use

Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from our users. The Shloka app is designed to provide spiritual content without requiring or collecting any personal data such as:
  • Names or contact information
  • Email addresses or phone numbers
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Any other personally identifiable information

Content Data

Our app retrieves spiritual content (shlokas, verses, and related materials) from our backend servers. This content is:

  • Spiritual texts
  • Educational and religious content
  • Retrieved anonymously without user identification
  • Cached locally on your device for offline access

Internet Permission

The app requires internet access solely to:

  • Download spiritual content from our backend servers
  • Update content when new materials are available
  • Ensure you have access to the latest verses and translations

No user data is transmitted to our servers during these operations.

Data Storage

Local Storage

The app may store downloaded content locally on your device for offline access. This includes:

  • Spiritual texts and verses
  • Translations and transliterations
  • App settings and preferences

All locally stored data is:

  • Limited to app functionality
  • Not shared with third parties
  • Automatically removed when you uninstall the app

No Cloud Storage

We do not store any user data in the cloud or on remote servers.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services that collect personal information. We do not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage providers for user data

Data Sharing

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not share, sell, or transfer any personal data to third parties.

Data Security

While we do not collect personal information, we implement appropriate technical measures to:

  • Secure the content delivery from our servers
  • Protect the integrity of spiritual content
  • Ensure reliable app functionality

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date." Continued use of the app after any modifications constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Contact Information

Email: shlokaapps@gmail.com

Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • Google Play Store requirements
  • Android app guidelines
  • General data protection principles

Summary: Shloka is a simple, privacy-focused application that provides spiritual content without collecting any personal information from users. We only use internet access to deliver content, and no user data is collected, stored, or shared.
